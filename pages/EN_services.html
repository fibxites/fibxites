Services

FIBXITES designs and developes responsive websites at front-end level, both English and Spanish, in order your service and/or product is known internationally. FIBXITES focuses on clients who seek:

personal website

To establish their own personal brand; for instance, scientists, politicians, celebrities, professors.

portafolio website

To showcase their professional work with the goal of winning clients; for instance, architects, artists, photographers.

business website

To have an online presence so people can learn about a company, their employees, products, services, and culture; for instance, labs, accounting firms, service providers (plumbers, HVAC, electricians, etc.), restaurants, beauty salons, and law offices.
